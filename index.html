Featured product

SmartRunFarm

A mobile farm management app for Kenyan dairy farmers. Herd records, co-operative milk deliveries, expenses, breeding, and health — all in one place.

Built for how Kenyan dairy farms actually work.

SmartRunFarm connects the moving parts of a dairy farm — herd, milk, breeding, health, and finance — with role-based access for owners, managers, workers, and veterinarians.

  • SMS import for Githunguri & Ndumberi-style co-op delivery messages
  • Manual delivery fallback when SMS isn't permitted — multiple pickups per day
  • Full lifecycle: calf → heifer → breeding → lactating / dry
  • Breeding workflow aligned to dairy practice, with timed reminders
  • Feed expenses broken down by herd group

Core capabilities

◆

Herd & lifecycle

Register cows, calves, heifers, and bulls. Full lifecycle — calf → heifer → breeding → first calving → lactating / dry — with tag-based identity, breed, value, and status. Record sales and deaths; dashboard shows herd counts and total value.

○

Milk & co-op integration

Automatic SMS import of co-op delivery messages on Android. Manual fallback for farms without SMS permission — multiple pickups per day (AM, PM, etc.). Per-cow tracking, monthly totals, daily trends, MoM/YoY change, milk targets, and a top producers ranking. Milk price rates published from co-op SMS.

◇

Breeding & reproduction

Record insemination (bull details, date). Confirm or rule out pregnancy. Record calving (live calf, stillbirth, etc.). Mark dry. Smart reminders for heat checks, pregnancy tests, dry-off, and steaming-up — due today or later, never backfilled.

+

Health

Per-cow health records — condition, vet, treatment, notes. Recent health surfaced on the dashboard. Veterinarians get a multi-farm view of health history across assigned farms and can record veterinary expenses only.

¤

Finance

Expenses by category — feeds (with type, supplier, herd group), veterinary, utilities, and more. Other income (sales, grants). Profit/loss with month-over-month trend and expense breakdown. Cow sales auto-recorded as income.

▤

Reports & account

PDF reports for animal health, feeding, monthly farm summary, and breeding. Report requests delivered by email. Profile setup, co-operative selection, password and terms management.

Role-based access

Role What they see and do
Owner Full farm view, expenses, reports, and settings
Manager Day-to-day recording — breeding, milk, herd
Worker Per-cow milk entry only
Veterinary Multi-farm picker, health history, and vet expenses
Admin Onboard vets, publish milk prices from SMS

Who it's for

  • Small & medium dairy farms tracking animals, milk sold to co-ops, and profitability
  • Owners & managers who need dashboards, reports, and full control
  • Farm workers focused on daily milk recording
  • Veterinarians serving multiple farms — health history and vet expenses only
  • Co-op-linked farmers (e.g. Githunguri Dairy, Ndumberi Dairy) who receive delivery SMS
