SmartRunFarm Privacy Policy

Last updated: 21 May 2026 · Effective date: 21 May 2026

FutureBridge Solutions Limited (“we”, “us”, or “our”) operates the SmartRunFarm mobile application (“App”) and related backend services (“Service”). We are registered and operate in Kenya.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, share, and protect information when you use the App on Android devices. By creating an account, logging in, or using the App, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, do not use the App.

1. Who this policy applies to

This policy applies to users of SmartRunFarm, including:

  • Farm owners and managers
  • Farm workers
  • Veterinary users with access to assigned farms
  • Administrators who create or manage accounts

The App is intended for business and farm management use, not for children.

2. Information we collect

2.1 Account and profile information

When you register or complete your profile, we may collect:

  • Username
  • Password (stored in hashed form on our servers; we do not store plain-text passwords)
  • Name (first name, where provided)
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Farm assignment and role (e.g. owner, manager, worker, veterinary)
  • Milk delivery cooperative selection (e.g. Githunguri Dairy, Ndumberi Dairy), where applicable
  • Terms of use acceptance and version

2.2 Farm and livestock data you enter

To provide farm management features, the App stores data you submit, including:

  • Cow, calf, and heifer records (tags, breeds, values, status, dates)
  • Milk production and co-operative delivery records (litres, dates, optional pickup labels such as AM or PM)
  • Breeding events (insemination, pregnancy, calving)
  • Animal health records (conditions, treatments, veterinary notes)
  • Expenses and income (categories, amounts, descriptions, feed details)
  • Milk price and rate information used for calculations
  • Reports and related farm operational data

You are responsible for the accuracy of data you enter.

2.3 SMS messages (optional — Android only)

If you grant SMS permission on Android, the App may read SMS messages on your device to support optional features:

  • Milk delivery import: Messages from your selected milk delivery cooperative (filtered by sender address) to extract delivery date and quantity.
  • Milk price import (authorized administrator users only): Messages from the FRESHADAIRY sender line to extract published milk rates.
Important:
  • SMS access is optional. If you deny permission, you can still use the App and manually enter co-op milk deliveries.
  • We do not use SMS permission to read personal text messages unrelated to milk co-operative operations.
  • Parsed delivery data (date, litres, and message text needed for duplicate detection) may be sent to our servers and stored with your farm account.
  • SMS import is not available on all platforms; on Android it requires your explicit permission.

We do not collect location, contacts, photos, microphone, or camera data through the App based on current permissions.

2.4 Device and technical information

We may automatically collect limited technical information needed to operate the Service, such as:

  • Device type and operating system version (as transmitted through normal app and API communication)
  • App version
  • Authentication tokens (to keep you signed in securely)
  • Error and performance-related logs where needed for reliability and security

2.5 Local preferences on your device

The App may store preferences on your device (for example privacy display settings or SMS permission status) using local storage. This data remains on your device unless sent to our servers as part of normal App use.

3. How we use your information

We use collected information to:

  • Create and manage user accounts and authenticate logins
  • Operate farm dashboards, records, and reports
  • Import or record milk deliveries and calculate milk-related income
  • Manage breeding workflows and send email reminders (for example pregnancy tests and dry-off) to farm users who have email addresses on file
  • Record expenses, income, and profitability views
  • Support veterinary access to health and veterinary-related expenses on assigned farms only
  • Prevent duplicate imports, maintain data integrity, and improve reliability
  • Comply with law, enforce our terms, and protect the security of the Service

We do not sell your personal information.

4. Legal basis for processing (Kenya)

We process personal data in accordance with applicable law, including the Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019, where it applies. Depending on the processing activity, we rely on:

  • Performance of a contract (providing the App and Service you signed up for)
  • Legitimate interests (security, fraud prevention, and service improvement)
  • Consent (for example SMS permission and terms acceptance)
  • Legal obligations (where retention or disclosure is required by law)

5. How we share information

We may share information only as follows:

5.1 Service providers

With trusted third parties that help us run the Service, such as cloud hosting, database, and email delivery providers. These parties may only use data to perform services for us and must protect it appropriately.

5.2 Within your farm organisation

Users on the same farm account (for example managers, workers, and assigned veterinarians) may see farm data according to their role permissions.

5.3 Administrators

Authorized system administrators may access account and farm data to create users, assign farms, troubleshoot issues, publish milk prices, and maintain the platform.

5.4 Legal requirements

We may disclose information if required by Kenyan law, court order, or a lawful government request, or to protect rights, safety, and security.

We do not share SMS content with advertisers.

6. Data storage and security

  • Data is stored on secure cloud-hosted systems operated for SmartRunFarm.
  • We use reasonable technical and organizational measures (such as access controls, authentication, and encrypted connections where supported) to protect data.
  • No method of transmission or storage is completely secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Data retention

  • We retain account and farm data while your account is active and as needed to provide the Service.
  • If you delete your account through the App (Account → Delete My Account and Data, with password confirmation), we delete your user account and associated app data linked to your farm account, and you are signed out.
  • Some information may be retained for a limited period where necessary for legal compliance, security, backups, dispute resolution, or audit purposes, after which it is deleted or anonymized where possible.

8. Your rights

Under the Kenya Data Protection Act and our practices, you may have the right to:

  • Be informed about how we use your data (this policy)
  • Access personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate profile or contact information
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data (via the in-app deletion feature)
  • Withdraw consent for SMS access at any time through Android Settings (the App continues to work with manual milk entry)
  • Object to or restrict certain processing, where applicable
  • Lodge a complaint with the Office of the Data Protection Commissioner (Kenya)

To exercise your rights, contact us using the details in Section 12. We may need to verify your identity before responding.

9. Children’s privacy

SmartRunFarm is not directed at children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided data, contact us and we will take appropriate steps to delete it.

10. International transfers

If our service providers process data outside Kenya, we take steps to ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, consistent with applicable data protection requirements.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version with a new “Last updated” date. If changes are material, we may notify you through the App or by email where appropriate. Continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy.
